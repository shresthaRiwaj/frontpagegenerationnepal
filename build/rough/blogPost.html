Buying a New Printer: Everything You Should Know

Author

April 1, 1997

When it comes to the specifications, the Acer Aspire 5 2021 closely resembles the aforementioned HP Notebook 15 EQ2115AU. It too has got AMD Ryzen 5 5500U processor and 8GB of DDR4 RAM on its system. But unlike the HP Notebook, the laptop RAM on this device can be expanded up to 32GB. And that ensures a fair bit of futureproofing. The storage side is taken care of by a 256GB Solid State Drive (SSD) but you can expand it on your need by tossing in an HDD. And the good thing is Acer has allocated a dedicated slot for it. As far as the display is concerned, you get a 15.6-inch display with an IPS panel. The screen features Full-HD resolution and is sufficiently bright with 250 nits of brightness. And talking about the keyboard, there is a Full-sized chiclet keyboard with keyboard backlighting as well. But the most impressive of all has to be its battery. Acer has included a sizable battery on this device, which can back up you for a whopping 10 hours. So if you are a student or an office employee, you may consider this one since it avoids the need to carry your bulky power adapters and chargers.
